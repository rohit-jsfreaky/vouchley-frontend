## About the service

Spamgourmet is one of the original alias-based disposable services. Users register a master account and then create disposable forwarding addresses on the fly using a `word.count.user@spamgourmet.com` syntax. The "count" portion is the number of messages the alias will forward before going silent.

## Typical use

Privacy-conscious users who want disposable per-service email aliases that forward to their real inbox for a fixed number of messages.

## Why block at signup

Spamgourmet addresses are explicitly disposable, even when forwarding works. Block at signup unless your product audience is dominated by privacy-first power users.
