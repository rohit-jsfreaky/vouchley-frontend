## About the service

Outlook.com is Microsoft's consumer webmail service, the successor to Hotmail. It is a legitimate free provider used by hundreds of millions of users for personal email.

## Typical use

General personal email, particularly common with Windows users and longtime Hotmail account holders who migrated.

## Why you should NOT block @outlook.com signups

Treat the same as Gmail — score normally and watch for the same alias-style abuse tricks. Hotmail and Outlook.com between them cover a meaningful portion of the global consumer email base; blocking them is functionally blocking a large fraction of the legitimate user pool.
