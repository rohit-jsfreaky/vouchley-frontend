## Pricing model

Vouchley runs on **simple monthly plans**. Each plan adds its credit allotment to your balance every billing cycle. Anything you don't use rolls over and never expires, so a quiet month is never wasted and a spike just draws down your rollover balance.

- 1 fresh verification = 1 credit
- Cache hit (same email + IP within 30 days) = 0 credits
- Unused credits roll over and never expire

## Pricing FAQ

### Do unused credits expire?
No. Each plan adds its credits every billing cycle, and anything you don't use rolls over and never expires. A slow month is never wasted.

### What happens if my balance hits zero?
The API returns `402 Payment Required` instead of running the check. No overages, no surprise bills. Your balance refills on your next billing cycle, or you can upgrade your plan.

### Are there volume discounts?
Yes, built into the plans. Pro saves 23% per credit vs Starter. Scale is the best rate. For genuinely enterprise volume (1M+ verifications/month), reach out — we'll quote.

### What payment methods are accepted?
Card payments are processed through Dodo Payments (our Merchant of Record). Dodo handles VAT, GST, and sales tax depending on your location. We never see card numbers or bank details.

### Are refunds available?
Yes, for unused credits within 14 days of purchase. Email `hello@getrevlio.com` and we'll reverse the charge through Dodo. Once credits are consumed, they're non-refundable (except where required by law).

## Why simple monthly plans

Signup-verification traffic is bursty — a launch, a viral post, a spam wave. Vouchley's rollover model means quiet months bank credits and busy months draw them down, so you're never over-paying for an idle month or slamming into a hard cap mid-spike.
