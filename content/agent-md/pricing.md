## Pricing FAQ

### Do credits really never expire?
Yes. There is no expiration date on Vouchley credits. If you buy a Starter pack today and use it slowly over two years, those credits are still there. This is a hard product commitment, not a "subject to terms" line.

### What happens if my balance hits zero?
The API returns `402 Payment Required` instead of running the check. No overages, no surprise bills. Top up another pack or enable auto-refill (Pro plan) to keep flowing.

### Can I stack multiple packs?
Yes. Buying Starter today and Pro next month just adds the credits together. There is no concept of "current plan" — only your credit balance.

### Are there volume discounts?
Yes, built into the pack pricing. Pro saves 15% per credit vs Starter. Scale is the best rate. For genuinely enterprise volume (1M+ verifications/month), reach out — we'll quote.

### What payment methods are accepted?
Card payments are processed through Dodo Payments (our Merchant of Record). Dodo handles VAT, GST, and sales tax depending on your location. We never see card numbers or bank details.

### Are refunds available?
Yes, for unused credits within 14 days of purchase. Email `hello@getrevlio.com` and we'll reverse the charge through Dodo. Once credits are consumed, they're non-refundable (except where required by law).

## Why credit packs instead of subscriptions

Most signup-verification traffic is bursty — a launch, a viral post, a spam wave. Subscription pricing forces you to either over-pay for an idle month or hit a cap mid-spike. Credit packs scale with what you actually use.
