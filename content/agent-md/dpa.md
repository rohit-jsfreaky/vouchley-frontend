# Data Processing Addendum — Vouchley

> Our DPA is available on request for business customers with GDPR or similar obligations.

**Document ID:** DPA-2026-04
**Effective:** April 19, 2026
**Last updated:** April 19, 2026
**Canonical URL:** https://vouchley.getrevlio.com/dpa

---

A Data Processing Addendum (DPA) formalises the roles and obligations between you (the data controller) and Vouchley (the data processor) when you run verifications on personal data of your own users.

## Availability

Our DPA covers the commitments in Article 28 of the GDPR, including:

- Subject matter, nature, and duration of processing
- Categories of data subjects and personal data
- Confidentiality and security of processing
- Sub-processor disclosure and approval (see the list in our [Privacy Policy](https://vouchley.getrevlio.com/privacy))
- Data-subject rights support (access, deletion, export)
- International transfer mechanisms (Standard Contractual Clauses)
- Breach notification within 72 hours
- Audit rights on reasonable notice

## How to request it

Because we sign DPAs one customer at a time during this early phase, we don't yet publish a click-through version. Email `privacy@getrevlio.com` from the address on your Vouchley account and we'll send the current PDF, pre-signed, within two business days. We're also happy to counter-sign your own template if it's substantively equivalent.

## Self-serve version

We'll publish a self-serve, click-to-accept DPA here once we have enough business customers to justify the review overhead. Until then, thanks for your patience.
