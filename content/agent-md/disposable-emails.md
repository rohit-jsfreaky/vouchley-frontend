## Disposable & temporary email services

These are services designed to bypass signup or avoid follow-up email. Block at signup.

- **[mailinator.com](https://vouchley.getrevlio.com/disposable-emails/mailinator-com)** — Mailinator (since 2003), public-inbox throwaway service
- **[10minutemail.com](https://vouchley.getrevlio.com/disposable-emails/10minutemail-com)** — 10MinuteMail (since 2007), self-destructing 10-minute inboxes
- **[guerrillamail.com](https://vouchley.getrevlio.com/disposable-emails/guerrillamail-com)** — Guerrilla Mail (since 2006), 1-hour disposable inboxes
- **[yopmail.com](https://vouchley.getrevlio.com/disposable-emails/yopmail-com)** — YOPmail (since 2004), French-origin public-inbox service
- **[maildrop.cc](https://vouchley.getrevlio.com/disposable-emails/maildrop-cc)** — Maildrop (since 2013), no-signup disposable inboxes by Heluna
- **[tempmail.org](https://vouchley.getrevlio.com/disposable-emails/tempmail-org)** — Temp Mail, mobile-app-distributed disposable email network
- **[throwawaymail.com](https://vouchley.getrevlio.com/disposable-emails/throwawaymail-com)** — ThrowAwayMail, 48-hour self-destructing inboxes

- **[mintemail.com](https://vouchley.getrevlio.com/disposable-emails/mintemail-com)** — MintEmail, 3-hour disposable email service
- **[spamgourmet.com](https://vouchley.getrevlio.com/disposable-emails/spamgourmet-com)** — Spamgourmet (since 2000), alias-based forwarding
- **[anonaddy.com](https://vouchley.getrevlio.com/disposable-emails/anonaddy-com)** — AnonAddy (now addy.io), open-source email aliasing
- **[simplelogin.io](https://vouchley.getrevlio.com/disposable-emails/simplelogin-com)** — SimpleLogin, email aliasing acquired by Proton
