## The story

We started Vouchley because we were tired of tools that promised the world and delivered bloated, brittle software. We wanted something solid. Something that felt like a ledger, not a toy.

Our approach is simple: focus on the core problem, ignore the noise, and charge a fair price for a tool that just works. We aren't here to disrupt your workflow; we're here to anchor it.

## Our tenets

### Ship things we'd use
We don't build features for the sake of marketing. If it doesn't solve a real problem for us, it doesn't ship.

### Boring tech
Innovation should happen in the user experience, not the infrastructure. We rely on proven, stable technologies — Postgres, Redis, FastAPI, Next.js. No bleeding-edge frameworks. No "we use this because it's cool."

### Price fairly
No dark patterns. No sudden price hikes. We charge what the tool is worth, and we aim to deliver ten times that value. Credit packs over subscriptions. No expiration tricks. No "enterprise quote" pages.

## What we're not

- We are **not** a 50-person company. We are one person, shipping from India, who answers your emails personally.
- We are **not** SOC 2 / HIPAA / ISO 27001 certified. If your procurement team requires these, we're honest about not being the right fit yet.
- We are **not** trying to build a fraud-decisioning platform. Vouchley does signup verification well. For payments fraud, content abuse, or account takeover protection, look at a full-stack vendor like Sift.

## What we are

- A focused product that does one job: scoring signups in real time so you can keep fake accounts out of your database.
- A predictable, transparent operation: published pricing, no contract, sign up and start in five minutes.
- A long-term commitment to staying small, opinionated, and useful — not raising a round and selling out the original buyer.
